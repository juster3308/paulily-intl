The Process

Uncompromising Craft

Every PAULILY piece passes through 47 individual steps — from raw material selection to final inspection. No shortcuts. No compromises.

01

Material Selection

We source only full-grain leather from certified tanneries in Italy and China's Shandong province. Each hide is hand-inspected for surface consistency, grain density, and natural character.

02

Hand Cutting & Stitching

Master artisans cut each pattern by hand using traditional Chinese shears. Double-reinforced saddle stitching ensures structural integrity that outlasts machine-sewn alternatives by a factor of three.

03

Hardware & Finishing

Custom-milled brass and PVD-coated titanium hardware — designed to develop a living patina over years of use. Every clasp, every buckle, every edge is polished through seven progressive stages.

Our Heritage

A Brand Born
from Conviction

PAULILY was founded in Shanghai with a singular conviction: that Chinese craftsmanship deserves the same global reverence as its European counterparts. We do not imitate — we originate.

Our design philosophy draws from the tension between Eastern aesthetic discipline and Western architectural precision. The result is a product that speaks quietly but carries unmistakable authority.
